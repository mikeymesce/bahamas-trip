Lighthouse Beach — Full Day Trip

Adventure

THE bucket list beach of Eleuthera. ~2 hour drive south, last 3 miles on rough dirt road. Endless pink sand, nearly deserted. Atlantic on one side, Caribbean on the other. Hidden caves, great shelling, excellent snorkeling.

Pro tip: Leave early. Pack at least 1 gallon of water per person, food, sunscreen, snorkel gear. Need decent car clearance. Do NOT drive up the final hill — park at the bottom and walk. This is a full day commitment but absolutely worth it.

Bonus Ideas

Optional

Sunset Boat Charter

Private 2-hour sunset cruise. Call Scott Bethel (242-468-1426) out of Governors Harbour, or for something upscale try The Cove Eleuthera (866-644-4452) — $550/hr includes charcuterie and wine. Budget $500–$1,100 depending on the vibe you want.

Romantic

💡 Book a day or two after you arrive — ask locals for recommendations too. The best operators aren't always online.

Optional

Stargazing on the Beach

Eleuthera has almost zero light pollution. Bring a blanket to Bird Point Beach after dinner any night and just look up. Download a stargazing app before the trip.

Romantic
Optional

Queen's Highway Scenic Drive

The whole island is one road. Pick a morning with no plans, drive with no destination, and stop at random beaches. You'll find spots no guidebook mentions. Keep your eyes open for roadside coconut stands.
