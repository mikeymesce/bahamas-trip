Arrival Day

Tuesday, May 5

11:15 AM

Land at Governor's Harbour

Wheels down in paradise. Pick up the car (arranged through Mike's cousin) near GHB airport.

Stop at Burrows One Stop or Eleuthera Supply (both 5-8 min drive). Stock up on breakfast/lunch supplies + bottled water — don't drink the tap!

Check in at Shooting Star at Bird of Paradise. 2 bed / 3 bath, infinity pool, beachfront pink sand. Explore Bird Point Beach right out front — snorkel gear included.

Evening

Dinner at Tippy's

Steps from the cottage — easy first night. Great food, island vibes.

2

First Full Day

Wednesday, May 6

Morning

Breakfast at the Cottage

Cook up something good in the full kitchen. Coffee on the patio with an ocean view.

What's the vibe today?

Mid-day

Glass Window Bridge

Drive north to see where the dark Atlantic meets the turquoise Caribbean — one of the most photographed spots in the Bahamas.

Afternoon

Queen's Baths + Preacher's Cave

Natural tidal pools carved into the rock, then explore the historic cave where shipwreck survivors sheltered in 1648.

All Day

French Leave Beach

Massive stretch of pink sand right near Governors Harbour. One of the best beaches in the Caribbean. Bring a book, a towel, and zero ambition.

Evening

Dinner at 1648

Waterfront dining with sunset cocktails. Named after the year Eleuthera was first settled.

3

Second Full Day

Thursday, May 7

Morning

Breakfast at the Cottage

Another morning in paradise. Take your time.

What's the vibe today?

Mid-day

Jet Skis & Kayaks at Buccaneer Club

Rent jet skis or kayaks and hit the water. They also have snorkel gear and paddle boards.

Afternoon

Snorkel a Remote Beach

Try Twin Coves — two small coves connected by a sand bridge. Amazing snorkeling in the northern cove. 5-10 min from the cottage.

All Day

Ten Bay Beach

Calm, shallow, crystal-clear water on the Caribbean side. 20 min south. Perfect for wading, floating, and doing absolutely nothing. Great snorkeling along the rock ledges too.

Evening

Dinner at Pascal's

Infinity pool with a swim-up cocktail bar. Yes, really. Come for the food, stay for the pool.

4

Last Full Day

Friday, May 8

Morning

Beach Morning

Last full day of beach mornings. Soak it in.

Afternoon

Explore Governors Harbour

Walk through the charming colonial town. Colorful buildings, friendly locals, maybe grab a souvenir.

6 PM – Late

FRIDAY NIGHT FISH FRY

THIS IS THE THING. Cupid's Cay — 6 PM to midnight. Fresh grilled fish, cold Kaliks, live Caribbean music, the whole island comes out. Don't miss this.

Know Before You Go

💧

No Tap Water

Buy bottled. Stock up Day 1.

🦟

Bug Spray

Picaridin for no-see-ums. Worst at dusk.

🚗

Drive on the LEFT

British rules. Stay alert.

⚡

Expect Outages

Power, water, internet can be spotty. Island life.

🍳

Cook In, Dine Out

Breakfast & lunch at cottage. Dinners out.
