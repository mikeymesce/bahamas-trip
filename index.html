Practical Tips

  • Don't drink tap water — buy bottled
  • Bug spray with Picaridin for no-see-ums
  • Drive on the LEFT side of the road
  • Expect occasional power/water/internet outages
  • Cook breakfast/lunch at cottage, dinners out

Culture & Customs

  • Bahamians are warm and friendly — greet people with "good morning/afternoon" before asking anything
  • Tipping: 15-20% at restaurants (same as the US). Tip taxi drivers, hotel staff, tour guides
  • The Bahamas uses the Bahamian dollar (BSD) — pegged 1:1 to USD. US dollars accepted everywhere
  • Dress is casual but cover up when leaving the beach (no swimsuits in town/restaurants)
  • Eleuthera is very safe but use common sense — lock the car, don't leave valuables on the beach
  • Sundays are quiet — many shops and restaurants close. Plan accordingly
  • Island time is real — things move slower here. Relax into it
  • Fresh conch is a local delicacy — try conch salad, cracked conch, conch fritters
  • Kalik is the national beer — order it cold at the Fish Fry
