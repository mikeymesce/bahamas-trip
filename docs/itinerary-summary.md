## WHAT TO CONSIDER ADDING

### Activities Not Yet on the Itinerary
1. **Lighthouse Beach** — THE bucket list beach of Eleuthera. Endless pink sand, nearly deserted, Atlantic meets Caribbean side by side. Full day trip (~2 hrs each way, rough road). Pack food/water. Best beach on the island but needs a dedicated day.

2. **Horseback riding on the beach** — Oceanview Farm offers morning beach rides. Could be a great surprise for Morgan.

3. **Snorkeling tour** — Hire a local guide for a boat snorkel trip to reefs you can't reach from shore. Ask at Buccaneer Club or the cottage.

4. **Ocean Hole in Rock Sound** — Natural blue hole, swimming allowed. ~45 min south. Could combine with a beach day.

5. **Leon Levy Native Plant Preserve** — Botanical garden in Governors Harbour. Low-key afternoon activity.

6. **Sunset cruise** — Ask locally about boat charters for a sunset ride. Romantic.
